About

Nathan Ankomah-Mensah is a 2nd year Master Student at Purdue University studying Electrical & Computer Engineering. He works as an Engineering intern at The MITRE Corporation year-round. His specialty is in the Internet of Things development and Machine Learning implementation. He completed his Bachelors in Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering, as well as a minor in Mathematics and Computer science from the University of Rhode Island in 2019. Nathan is a member of Eta Kappa Nu, National Society of Black Engineers, and an Alumni of Theta Tau. Outside of Engineering, he enjoys comics, video games, and mentoring.
